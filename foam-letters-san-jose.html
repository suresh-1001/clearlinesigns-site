About Foam Letters

What Are Foam Letters
and When Do They Make Sense?

Foam letters are dimensional characters cut from high-density urethane (HDU) or EPS foam, then finished with paint, vinyl wrap, or a hard laminate coating. The result looks and photographs nearly identically to metal or acrylic dimensional letters — with two key differences: they weigh significantly less, and they cost less to fabricate.

For indoor applications — lobby walls, reception areas, conference rooms, retail feature walls — foam letters are a smart alternative to metal. They mount with standard hardware, hold paint cleanly, and can be finished in any color including metallic and brushed effects that mimic aluminum or bronze at a lower price point. For large-format letters above 18 inches tall, foam is often the practical choice purely due to weight.

Outdoor foam letters require a hard-coat finish and UV-resistant paint to hold up against California weather, but properly sealed HDU foam performs well on building exteriors and monument signs. We specify the right material and finish for each application — interior or exterior, temporary or permanent.

Finish Options

Types of Foam Letters
We Fabricate & Install

Painted Foam Letters

HDU foam CNC-cut to shape and finished with multiple coats of exterior-grade paint. Available in any color — flat, satin, gloss, or metallic. The most cost-effective dimensional letter option for indoor lobbies and retail walls.

Vinyl-Wrapped Foam Letters

Foam letter faces and returns wrapped in cast vinyl for precise color matching to brand standards. Metallic, brushed, and specialty vinyl films give foam letters a premium finished look that rivals aluminum at a lower cost.

Hard-Coat Foam Letters

EPS or HDU foam with a rigid polyurea or fiberglass hard-coat applied before painting. Required for outdoor installations — the coating seals the foam completely against moisture, UV, and impact for long-term exterior durability.

Foam Logo Cutouts

Full logo shapes — not just letters — cut from foam and finished to match brand colors. Common for lobby feature walls, trade show displays, and retail brand installations where the logo includes icons or graphic elements beyond text.

Oversized Foam Letters

Letters above 24 inches tall where metal would be prohibitively heavy or expensive. Foam scales well to large formats — 3, 4, even 5-foot letters for building signs, stage backdrops, and large retail environments.

Foam Letters for Events

Temporary or reusable foam letter displays for trade shows, product launches, and corporate events. Lightweight for easy transport and setup, finished to look sharp in photos and on camera.
