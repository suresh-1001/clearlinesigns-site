About Vinyl Banners

Custom Vinyl Banners
for Every Business Need

Vinyl banners are one of the most cost-effective signs you can buy — high-visibility, full-color, and deployable in days rather than weeks. For San Jose businesses, they fill the gap between permanent signage and short-term promotions: grand opening announcements, sale events, construction site branding, trade show backdrops, real estate listings, and outdoor advertising along busy corridors.

Standard vinyl banners are printed on 13 oz or 18 oz scrim vinyl, UV-laminated for outdoor durability, and finished with sewn hems and brass grommets for secure mounting. Heavier applications — fence wraps on construction sites, rooftop banners, and long-span installations — use mesh vinyl that vents wind load without compromising visibility. We also produce retractable banners and fabric backdrops for trade show and event use.

Most vinyl banners don't require a sign permit in San Jose for temporary placement, which means faster deployment. We'll flag the rare exceptions — such as long-term banners on building exteriors or within specific zoning districts — before production starts so you're not caught off guard.

Banner Options

Types of Vinyl Banners
We Print & Install

Outdoor Vinyl Banners

Standard 13 oz or 18 oz scrim vinyl, UV-laminated and grommeted for fences, building exteriors, storefronts, and street-facing installations. Built for California sun and wind.

Mesh Banners

Perforated vinyl that allows wind to pass through — the right call for construction fences, rooftop installations, and any banner that needs to span a long distance without risking damage.

Retractable Banner Stands

Portable pull-up banners for trade shows, lobbies, retail displays, and events. Prints ship fast, stands available in standard and wide formats. Easy to swap graphics when campaigns change.

Step & Repeat Banners

Branded event backdrops for press appearances, grand openings, award ceremonies, and corporate events. Available with stand hardware and carrying cases for repeat use.

Fabric Banners & Backdrops

Dye-sublimation printed fabric for a premium look at trade shows and events — no wrinkles, no glare, and folds into a compact carry bag for transport between venues.

Construction Site Banners

Fence wrap banners, project identification signs, and site branding for construction and development projects. Reinforced edges and heavy-duty grommets for long-duration outdoor exposure.
