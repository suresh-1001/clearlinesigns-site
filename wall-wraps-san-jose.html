About Wall Wraps

Custom Wall Wraps
for San Jose Businesses

A wall wrap is a large-format printed vinyl graphic applied directly to a wall surface. Unlike paint or wallpaper, wall wraps are produced digitally — which means full photographic quality, precise color matching to your brand, and limitless design options. They go up without construction, they come down without damaging the wall, and they transform a space in a day.

Wall wraps are one of the fastest-growing interior upgrades for San Jose tech offices, restaurants, gyms, and retail spaces. A mural-scale brand graphic in a lobby makes an impression that a painted accent wall never could. A full-wall nature scene in a restaurant changes the atmosphere of the entire room. A culture wall in a tech office tells your company story to every employee and visitor who walks by.

We handle every part of the process — concept and design, large-format printing, surface preparation, and professional installation. Most wall wrap projects for standard commercial spaces complete in one to two days. No general contractor required, no painting, no downtime beyond the installation window.

How It Works

From Concept to
Installed Wall Wrap

Most San Jose wall wrap projects go from first conversation to installed graphic in 1–2 weeks.

1
Step One

Free Consultation

We discuss your space, your goals, and your design direction. Share your wall dimensions, photos of the space, and any brand assets you have. We assess the wall surface and confirm the best vinyl product for your application.

2
Step Two

Design & Proof

We produce a scaled digital rendering showing exactly how your wall wrap will look in the space. You review and approve before anything goes to print. Revisions are included until you're satisfied with the design.

3
Step Three

Large-Format Printing

Your wall wrap is printed in panels on commercial wide-format equipment with UV-stable inks. Print turnaround is typically 3–5 business days. We inspect color and quality before scheduling installation.

4
Step Four

Professional Installation

Our crew preps the wall surface, applies the wrap in panels with precise seam alignment, and trims all edges cleanly. Most installations complete in a single day. We leave the space clean and ready to use.
