Frequently Asked Questions

San Jose Sign Company
— Common Questions Answered

How long does a sign permit take in San Jose?

Most sign permits in San Jose take 4–8 weeks through the Development Services Department. Simple wall signs are on the faster end; signs requiring structural review or electrical permits take longer. We start the permit process immediately after design approval so fabrication can run in parallel — minimizing total project time. See our San Jose permit timeline guide →

Do you handle landlord approval packages for San Jose storefront signs?

Yes — landlord approval is included in every exterior sign project. We prepare sign drawings, material specs, and a complete approval package in the format your property manager requires. Most landlord approvals come back within 1–2 weeks when the package is complete and professional.

Do interior signs require a permit in San Jose?

No — interior signs including lobby signs, ADA signs, office signs, and wayfinding do not require a City of San Jose sign permit. This means interior projects move faster: from approved proof to installed sign is typically 2–3 weeks with no permit wait.

What areas of San Jose do you serve?

We serve all of San Jose — Downtown, North San Jose, Willow Glen, Santana Row, East San Jose, Alum Rock, Cambrian, Blossom Valley, and Almaden. We also serve the surrounding cities including Santa Clara, Sunnyvale, Milpitas, Mountain View, Cupertino, Campbell, and Los Gatos.

How much do commercial signs cost in San Jose?

Commercial sign costs vary widely based on sign type, size, materials, and whether permits are required. A simple lobby sign starts in the hundreds; a full monument sign with permits and installation runs several thousand. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific project — contact us for a free estimate.

Can you match our brand colors exactly?

Yes. We work from your Pantone color codes, CMYK values, or brand guide files. For vinyl graphics and printed signs, we color-match digitally. For painted dimensional letters and metal signs, we use powder-coat and paint systems that can match most brand standards. Send us your brand guide and we'll confirm accuracy before fabrication.
