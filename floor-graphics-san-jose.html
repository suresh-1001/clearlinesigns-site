About Floor Graphics

Floor Graphics — An Underused
Branding Surface Most Businesses Ignore

Floor graphics turn the most-trafficked surface in your building into a branding and wayfinding tool. In a corporate lobby, a large-format floor decal with your logo creates an immediate impression that wall signage alone can't match — visitors are looking down the moment they walk through the door. In retail, floor graphics direct customers to featured products, promotions, and checkout areas. In medical and healthcare environments, floor arrows and zone markings reduce confusion and improve patient flow without requiring staff intervention.

The material matters as much as the design. Floor graphics require a specialized vinyl with a textured, slip-resistant laminate topcoat rated for foot traffic. The adhesive has to bond securely to the floor surface — whether that's polished concrete, ceramic tile, commercial carpet, or hardwood — without leaving residue when removed. We specify the right product for your surface and traffic level, not a one-size-fits-all material that fails prematurely or damages the floor on removal.

We've installed floor graphics in tech company lobbies, retail stores, and event venues throughout San Jose and Silicon Valley. Large-format installs — multi-panel seamless graphics covering hundreds of square feet — are a specialty. We handle the seaming, alignment, and surface prep that separates a professional install from a bubbled, peeling mess two weeks later.
