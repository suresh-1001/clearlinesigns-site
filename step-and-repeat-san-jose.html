About Step and Repeat Backdrops

What Is a Step and Repeat Backdrop
and When Do You Need One?

A step and repeat backdrop is a large printed panel — typically 8 to 10 feet wide — featuring your logo or a pattern of multiple logos repeated in a grid. The name comes from the print technique: one logo element is stepped across and repeated to fill the surface. The result is a branded wall that looks professional in every photo taken in front of it, whether that's a phone shot posted to Instagram or a press image that runs in a publication.

They're standard at corporate events, product launches, award ceremonies, fundraisers, trade show booths, and press conferences. For Silicon Valley companies, they show up at everything from startup demo days to large enterprise customer summits. If people will be photographed or recorded at your event, a step and repeat backdrop turns every one of those images into brand exposure.

We produce step and repeat backdrops on fabric or vinyl, sized to your event space, with hardware included. Everything ships in a carry case or can be picked up from San Jose. Setup takes under 10 minutes with no tools required.

Backdrop Options

Types of Step and Repeat Backdrops
We Design & Produce

Fabric Step and Repeat

Dye-sublimation printed on wrinkle-resistant polyester fabric. Crisp color, no glare in photos, and machine washable for reuse across multiple events. The most popular option for corporate and media events.

Vinyl Step and Repeat

High-resolution vinyl print on a tension frame or retractable stand. More rigid than fabric, handles outdoor conditions better, and is a cost-effective option for shorter-run events or budget-sensitive projects.

Backlit Fabric Backdrop

SEG (silicone edge graphics) fabric in an LED-lit aluminum frame. Your logos glow evenly from behind, making the backdrop pop in any lighting environment — especially effective on darker event floors.

Curved Backdrop

A curved aluminum frame gives the backdrop a concave shape, wrapping slightly around subjects for a more immersive branded look. Popular for product announcements and high-production event stages.

Custom Layout Backdrop

Not every backdrop needs a repeating logo grid. We produce full-bleed custom designs — scenic backgrounds, branded environments, or mixed logo/image layouts — for events with specific creative direction.

Retractable Backdrop Stand

A retractable banner stand scaled to backdrop width — 5 to 8 feet. Graphic retracts into the base for transport. More compact than a full frame kit, suited for smaller spaces or frequent travel.
