How It Works

From First Call to
Show-Ready Booth

Most trade show display orders follow this process. Timelines vary by product type — we'll confirm specifics when you reach out.

1
Step One

Tell Us Your Show

Share your show date, venue, booth size, and what display types you're considering. We'll confirm whether your timeline is achievable and recommend products that fit your booth footprint and budget.

2
Step Two

Design & Proof

Send us your logo, brand colors, and any messaging. We lay out the graphics to the exact dimensions of your chosen display and produce a digital proof. One round of revisions included before production starts.

3
Step Three

Production

Once the proof is approved, production begins. Typical production lead times: banner stands 3–5 business days, tension fabric and SEG displays 5–8 business days, complete booth kits 10–15 business days. We'll confirm your specific timeline.

4
Step Four

Delivery & Setup

Displays ship directly to your office or to the convention center in advance of your show date. All hardware includes setup instructions. Most displays are tool-free and set up in minutes. For Bay Area shows, local delivery coordination is available.
