About Fabric Displays

What Are Fabric Displays
and Why Do Businesses Choose Them?

Fabric displays use dye-sublimation printed polyester stretched over a lightweight aluminum frame. The graphic is either zipped on, tucked into a silicone edge channel (SEG — silicone edge graphics), or wrapped around the frame depending on the system. The result is a seamless, smooth surface with no visible hardware, no wrinkles, and color that stays accurate under bright event lighting and camera flashes.

Compared to vinyl, fabric displays are lighter, pack smaller, and don't crease in transit. They're the default choice for trade show booths, corporate event walls, retail environments, and anywhere a clean premium look matters. The hardware lasts for years — when you rebrand or run a new campaign, you swap the graphic, not the whole display.

We produce fabric displays for the full range of use cases: portable banner stands for a single-person tabletop exhibit, 10x10 and 10x20 SEG booth walls for convention floor setups, backlit LED frames for reception areas and retail, and large-format fabric backdrops for events across Silicon Valley. If you have hardware already, we can produce replacement graphics to fit.

Display Options

Types of Fabric Displays
We Design & Produce

SEG Fabric Displays

Silicone edge graphics slip into a routed channel on an aluminum frame, creating a perfectly taut, seamless surface. Available straight or curved, in sizes from 3-foot tabletop panels to 20-foot trade show walls.

Backlit LED Fabric Displays

An LED-illuminated aluminum frame with a translucent fabric graphic that glows evenly from behind. Maximum visibility on a crowded show floor, and highly effective in retail and corporate reception environments.

Tension Fabric Pop-Up Displays

Collapsible aluminum frame with a fabric graphic that zips or clips on. Folds into a carry bag in minutes. A workhorse booth display that looks sharp and sets up without tools.

Fabric Banner Stands

Retractable or tension fabric banner stands for single-display setups, tabletop exhibits, and retail environments. Fabric graphic gives a softer, more refined look than vinyl at comparable cost.

Fabric Backdrops & Event Walls

Large-format printed fabric panels on adjustable pipe-and-drape or aluminum frame systems. Used for step-and-repeat press walls, stage backdrops, product launch environments, and branded event spaces.

Fabric Table Covers

Fitted or stretch fabric table covers with full dye-sublimation printing. Fitted covers stay put during busy show hours, look tailored, and are machine washable for reuse across multiple events.
