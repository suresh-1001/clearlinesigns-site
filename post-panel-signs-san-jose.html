About Post & Panel Signs

What Are Post & Panel Signs
and Where They Work Best

A post and panel sign is a freestanding ground sign where one or two vertical posts support a flat panel displaying your business name, logo, or tenant directory. They're a cost-effective alternative to monument signs when the project budget, site conditions, or timeline don't support masonry construction — and they're faster to permit and install.

Post and panel signs are common at office parks, medical buildings, churches, schools, auto dealerships, and multi-tenant commercial properties across San Jose and Silicon Valley. The panel face can be a single tenant or a multi-tenant directory that accommodates tenant changes with swappable panels — which makes them a practical long-term choice for property managers.

Most exterior post and panel signs in San Jose require a sign permit covering height, setback from property lines, and total sign area. We handle the permit submittal through SJPermits.org, including site plan documentation. Concrete footings for post installation are part of our standard scope — we don't leave that to a separate contractor.

Sign Options

Post & Panel Sign Types
We Design & Install

Single-Post Panel Sign

One post centered on the back of the panel — compact footprint, clean look. Common for single-tenant identification at small office buildings, medical practices, and professional services in San Jose.

Double-Post (H-Frame) Sign

Two posts on either side of the panel for larger, wider signs. The most common format for multi-tenant directories and larger commercial property identification signs across Silicon Valley.

Multi-Tenant Directory Sign

Panel with swappable tenant name inserts — when a tenant moves in or out, only the insert changes, not the full sign structure. Ideal for property managers running office parks or medical buildings.

Decorative Post Signs

Finished aluminum or powder-coated steel posts with routed or carved panel faces — appropriate for upscale office parks, HOAs, hospitality properties, and institutional campuses where aesthetics matter alongside function.

Illuminated Post & Panel

LED-lit panel face for 24/7 visibility. Common for medical facilities, hotels, and commercial properties where nighttime identification is a requirement. Requires electrical coordination and permits for the illumination component.

Real Estate & Construction Signs

Temporary or semi-permanent post and panel signs for real estate listings, construction sites, and development projects. Fast production, ground-driven post option for temporary installations without concrete.
