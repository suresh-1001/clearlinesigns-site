About A-Frame Signs

A-Frame Signs — Small Footprint,
Big Impact on Foot Traffic

An A-frame sign — also called a sandwich board or sidewalk sign — is one of the most effective tools a street-level business has. It sits right in the path of foot traffic, at eye level, with a message that changes as often as you need it to. For restaurants promoting a lunch special, a salon running a same-day discount, or a retailer directing shoppers off the main corridor, a well-designed A-frame does its job without requiring a sign permit in most San Jose sidewalk placements.

The right A-frame depends on how you plan to use it. Chalkboard-style frames give restaurants and cafes a hand-written, approachable feel that customers respond to. Snap-frame A-frames use interchangeable printed inserts — the frame stays on the sidewalk while you swap graphics for different promotions, days, or seasons. For businesses that want full brand impact, custom-printed acrylic or aluminum panel A-frames deliver a sharp, consistent look that matches your storefront signage.

We supply frames, design and print custom inserts, and can produce matching sets for multi-location businesses throughout San Jose and Silicon Valley. If your lease or property requires sidewalk sign approval, we can advise on the City of San Jose sidewalk encroachment permit process for A-frames on public sidewalks.

Sign Options

Types of A-Frame Signs
We Supply & Print

Printed Insert A-Frames

Aluminum or steel frames with custom-printed coroplast or foam board inserts. Sharp, full-color graphics at a low cost — ideal for promotions that run for weeks at a time.

Snap-Frame A-Frames

Frames with spring-loaded snap borders that hold interchangeable printed inserts. Change your message in seconds without tools — one frame, unlimited campaigns.

Chalkboard A-Frames

Classic black chalkboard panels on folding frames — wooden or aluminum. The handwritten look works especially well for restaurants, coffee shops, and boutique retail.

Rigid Panel A-Frames

Heavy-duty aluminum frames with permanently printed aluminum or acrylic panels. Used when the message doesn't change and a more permanent, polished look is needed.

Water-Base Weighted A-Frames

Hollow-base frames that fill with water or sand for stability in high-wind areas or on uneven surfaces — a practical choice for San Jose's outdoor dining areas and exposed sidewalks.

Custom Shaped & Branded Frames

Non-standard shapes, custom colors, and branded frame finishes for businesses that want their sidewalk sign to stand out as part of a cohesive storefront identity.
