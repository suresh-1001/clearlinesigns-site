Frequently Asked Questions

Los Gatos Sign Company
— Common Questions Answered

How long does a sign permit take in Los Gatos?

Most sign permits in Los Gatos take 3–6 weeks through the Community Development Department. We submit complete permit packages upfront — including site plans, sign drawings, and material specs — which minimizes back-and-forth and keeps the timeline tight. Fabrication runs in parallel once the permit is in review.

Do you handle landlord approval for Los Gatos storefront signs?

Yes — landlord approval is part of every exterior sign project. We prepare sign drawings, material samples, and a complete approval package in the format your property manager requires. Most approvals come back within 1–2 weeks when the package is professional and complete.

Do interior signs require a permit in Los Gatos?

No — interior signs including lobby signs, ADA signs, office signs, and wayfinding do not require a City of Los Gatos sign permit. Interior projects move fast: from approved proof to installed sign is typically 2–3 weeks with no permit wait.

Can you match our brand standards exactly?

Yes. We work from Pantone color codes, CMYK values, or brand guide files. For vinyl and printed signs, we color-match digitally. For painted dimensional letters and metal signs, we use powder-coat and paint systems that can match most brand standards. Send us your brand guide and we'll confirm accuracy before fabrication starts.
