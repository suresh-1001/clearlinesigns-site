About Office Reception Signs

Custom Reception Signs
for San Jose Offices

Your reception area is the first physical space a client, investor, or new hire walks into. The sign on that wall sets the tone for everything that follows. In San Jose's competitive tech and corporate environment, a well-executed reception sign is one of the highest-ROI investments a business can make in its physical space.

Office reception signs range from simple flat-cut acrylic letters to backlit dimensional logo panels and brushed metal installations. The right choice depends on your brand identity, the wall surface, the size of the space, and the visual weight you want to project. A seed-stage startup and a Series C company both need a reception sign — they just send different signals with different materials.

We've installed reception signs for tech companies, law firms, medical offices, and corporate headquarters across San Jose and Silicon Valley — including JFrog, H2O.ai, Netskope, Mirantis, and Fujitsu. Every project starts with a scaled rendering so you see exactly what you're getting before fabrication begins.

Sign Options

Types of Office Reception Signs
We Design & Install

Dimensional Acrylic Letters

Individual letters or logo shapes cut from colored acrylic and mounted with standoffs for depth and shadow. The most common reception sign format in Silicon Valley tech offices. Clean, modern, and cost-effective.

Acrylic Logo Panels

Your full logo printed or applied to a clear or colored acrylic backer panel, mounted with standoffs. Works well for complex logos with multiple colors or fine detail that's difficult to achieve in cut letters alone.

Brushed Metal Letters

Dimensional letters in brushed stainless steel or aluminum — the material choice for corporate headquarters, law firms, and established companies where the sign needs to communicate permanence and quality.

Backlit & Halo-Lit Signs

Illuminated reception signs with LED backlighting or halo-lit letters that glow from behind. High visual impact in both naturally lit and dimmer reception environments. Requires electrical rough-in planning.

Mixed Material Signs

Combinations of acrylic, metal, and printed elements — a brushed aluminum backer with dimensional acrylic letters on top, or a printed panel with metal standoff letters. Allows layered brand expression.

Wall Logo Graphics

High-resolution printed vinyl or cut vinyl logo applied directly to the wall. The fastest and most affordable reception sign option — good for early-stage companies or temporary spaces that need a professional look quickly.
