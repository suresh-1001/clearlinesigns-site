Options & Formats

Vehicle Graphic Options
We Design & Install

From a simple door logo to a full fleet program — here's what we offer and where each option fits.

Cut Vinyl Lettering

Single-color vinyl precision-cut to shape and applied directly to the vehicle surface. Business name, phone number, website, services list, license number — the standard for commercial service vehicles. Clean, legible, UV-stable for 5–7 years. Available in any color. Removes cleanly when the vehicle is retired or sold.

Most Common · Best Value

Logo & Color Graphics

Full-color printed vinyl graphics — logo, icon, or graphic element — combined with cut vinyl lettering. The result is a branded vehicle that goes beyond plain text. Common when your logo includes a complex icon or gradient that can't be reproduced in single-color cut vinyl. As seen on the MDE Electric, Octo Painting, and Natural Arbor Care vehicles above.

Full Color · Printed Vinyl

Partial Graphics / Panel Graphics

Printed vinyl panels covering a door, a side panel, or a trailer side — more coverage than simple lettering, less commitment than a full wrap. Common on trailers, box trucks, and large vans where the flat panel surface makes installation straightforward. The Pav Bhaji Hut and Natural Arbor Care trailers above are examples of this format.

Trailers · Box Trucks · Vans

Fleet Programs

Consistent branding across multiple vehicles — same template, same positioning, same install quality on every vehicle in the fleet. We supply a master template and apply it across 2, 5, or 20+ vehicles as your fleet grows. Volume pricing applies. The Aspen Fire & Security pair above is a straightforward example — same layout, applied to both vans on the same day.

Volume Pricing · Fleet-Ready

Magnetic Signs

30-mil magnetic vehicle signs for businesses that need to brand a personal vehicle part-time — on for work days, off for personal use. Available in standard sizes for car doors, truck doors, and van panels. A lower-cost option for owner-operators who aren't ready to commit to permanent vinyl. Sold in pairs.

Removable · Owner-Operators

Trailer & Box Truck Graphics

Trailers and box trucks offer the largest flat surface area of any commercial vehicle — and the most visible real estate for your brand. Full-color printed vinyl on trailer sides turns your equipment into a rolling billboard on Bay Area highways. High-visibility for service, food, and delivery businesses operating throughout Silicon Valley.

Fast Turnaround

Most cut vinyl vehicle lettering jobs are designed, proofed, and installed within 3–5 business days. For fleet orders of 5+ vehicles we'll confirm the schedule at the time of order.

California UV-Rated Vinyl

We use commercial-grade cast vinyl — not calendered film — for vehicle applications. Cast vinyl conforms to curved surfaces without lifting and holds its color and adhesion for 5–7 years in California sun. The difference matters on a vehicle that parks outside every day in a San Jose summer.

Proof Before Every Install

We send a digital proof showing your graphics positioned on a vehicle photo before we cut anything. Font size, spacing, placement — you see it all before the vinyl touches the vehicle. Changes at the proof stage cost nothing. Changes after installation cost significantly more.

Fleet Pricing & Template Management

Once your vehicle template is on file, reordering for new vehicles takes one conversation. We keep your approved layout and can re-cut and install on a new vehicle quickly. Fleet customers get volume pricing that scales with order size. We currently manage fleet templates for service companies throughout Santa Clara County.

How It Works

From Logo File to
Lettered Vehicle

Most vehicle lettering jobs are complete within a week. Here's the process.

1
Step One

Send Your Info & Logo

Tell us your vehicle make, model, and year, and send your logo file (vector preferred — AI, EPS, or SVG). Include what you want on the vehicle: business name, phone, website, services, license number. We'll handle the layout.

2
Step Two

Design & Proof

We position your graphics on a photo of your vehicle type and send a proof for approval. You see exactly where everything goes, at what size, in what color, before we cut anything. Revisions at this stage are free.

3
Step Three

Cut & Print

Once you approve the proof, we cut your vinyl and print any full-color elements. Production is typically 1–2 business days for standard lettering jobs. Complex multi-panel graphics may take an additional day.

4
Step Four

Installation

Bring your vehicle to our San Jose location or we can arrange mobile installation for fleet orders. Installation typically takes 2–4 hours for a standard lettering job. We clean the surface, apply the vinyl, and finish edges cleanly so nothing lifts in California sun or rain.
