About Vista System

Modular Signage That
Moves With Your Building

Vista System is a global leader in modular architectural wayfinding, used in hospitals, corporate campuses, universities, and government facilities worldwide. The core idea is simple: a sign frame that holds a swappable graphic insert. When a tenant moves, a department renames, or a room changes use, you replace the insert — not the sign.

For property managers running multi-tenant buildings in Silicon Valley, where companies scale and relocate constantly, that's not a minor convenience. It's the difference between a $40 insert swap and a $400 sign replacement, every time a suite changes hands. As an authorized Vista System dealer, Clear Line Signs supplies, specifies, and installs the full Vista product line — and we carry physical samples of every system so you can see and feel the materials before you commit.

We also use Vista's Vspec service on larger projects — a free sign schedule and specification package built from your architectural drawings, detailing every sign type, size, location, and quantity. It turns a multi-floor wayfinding project into an organized, presentable plan before a single sign is fabricated.

The Vista Product Line

Vista System Families
We Supply & Install

Vista — Classic Curved

The original Vista platform. Curved aluminum frames with narrow lips, available in a wide range of sizes — wall signs, flag signs, suspended signs, and freestanding directories, all sharing the same interchangeable insert system.

Vista Nova — New Curved Design

A refreshed curved profile with wider lips and a softer arch. Same rapid-change insert system as Classic Vista, with wall frames, directories, flag signs, and table signs in a more contemporary look.

Vista Square — Flat Modular

A flat-profile system with distinctive side panels for a clean architectural look. Vertical and horizontal wall frames, directories, suspended signs, table signs, floating wall-mounts, and unique 90° corner signs.

Vista Sharp — Frameless Modular

An almost invisible-frame design for a clean, screen-like appearance. Wall signs, directories, flag signs, suspended signs, and double-sided stands — graphics swap via side-channel or magnetic insert.

Vista ART — ADA Acrylic Signs

Grade 2 Braille, raised tactile characters, and required pictograms in a premium acrylic frame. Built to meet ADA and California CBC §11B-703 — stricter than federal ADA in several respects.

Vista Expand — Large Format

Post-and-panel and large directory signs for campus exteriors, parking structures, and multi-building wayfinding — built for outdoor durability.
