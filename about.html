Who We Are

A Local San Jose Sign Company — Not a Franchise

Clear Line Signs is a commercial signage company based in San Jose, California. We serve businesses across Silicon Valley — from tech startups in North San Jose to medical offices in Campbell, retail tenants in Sunnyvale, and corporate campuses throughout Santa Clara and the South Bay.

We're not a national chain. Not a franchise. Every project is managed locally, by the same person from the first call to the final installation. That matters when your sign permit stalls, your landlord has last-minute changes, or your opening date moves. You call us — not a 1-800 number.

Our work covers the full range of commercial signage: lobby signs, storefront signs, channel letters, monument signs, ADA-compliant signs, office sign packages, window graphics, wall graphics, and trade show displays. If a Bay Area business needs it on a wall, door, window, or building, we build it.

  • One team, start to finish. Design proof → fabrication → permit → installation. No handoffs, no miscommunication between vendors.
  • Local permit knowledge. We know San Jose's sign code, Santa Clara's review process, and what landlords in Silicon Valley office parks require in an approval package.
  • Commercial-grade materials. UV-stable vinyls, powder-coated aluminum, UL-listed LEDs — built to hold up outdoors in California's climate for years, not months.

How We Work

What You Can Expect on
Every Project

We work the same way on a $400 decal order as we do on a $25,000 monument sign program. These are the things we don't cut corners on.

Honest Quoting

We quote what the job actually costs — materials, labor, permit fees, and installation. No low-ball estimates that expand after approval. If your project requires a structural engineer or a specialty installer, we tell you upfront and include it in the quote.

Permit Expertise — Not Avoidance

Some sign companies steer clients away from sign types that require permits because permits are complicated. We don't. We handle permit submittals, engineering documentation, landlord approval packages, and City review management as a standard part of every project that requires it.

Timeline Accountability

We give you a realistic schedule at the start and communicate proactively when anything changes. Permit timelines, fabrication lead times, and install scheduling are all communicated clearly so your opening, your rebrand, or your move-in date doesn't depend on a sign company that doesn't return calls.

Material Standards

We don't substitute materials without telling you. If your quote calls for 3M cast vinyl, you get 3M cast vinyl — not a house-brand calendered film. Our outdoor signs use UV-stable, weather-resistant materials specified for California's climate and sun exposure, not the cheapest option that ships fast.

Single Point of Contact

You work with one person throughout the project. No sales handoff to production, no production handoff to install crew. When you email or call about your sign, you reach the person who knows your project — because it's the same person who quoted it, designed it, and is managing fabrication.

ADA Compliance by Default

Every room ID sign, restroom sign, and door sign we produce is built to California Title 24 requirements — not just federal ADA. We don't treat accessibility compliance as an upgrade. It's included in the quote and built into the production spec on every applicable sign type.
