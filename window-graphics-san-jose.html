About Window Graphics

What Window Graphics Can Do
for Your Business

Window graphics are one of the most versatile and cost-effective sign types available. A storefront with bare glass is a missed opportunity — that same glass can display your brand, communicate your services, attract foot traffic, or give your office the privacy it needs, all without touching the building structure.

In San Jose and across Silicon Valley, we install window graphics for retail storefronts, tech offices, medical practices, restaurants, and more. Applications range from a simple cut vinyl logo on a suite door to a full-color printed wrap covering an entire storefront window wall, to frosted privacy film across a conference room's glass partition.

Unlike most exterior signs, window graphics don't require a City of San Jose sign permit for most applications — which means faster turnaround and lower total cost. We handle design, printing, and installation from our San Jose base, typically completing projects in one to two weeks.

Types of Window Graphics

Every Window Application
We Design & Install

Different glass, different goals, different budgets — here's what we offer and what each is best for.

Full-Color Printed Vinyl

High-resolution digitally printed vinyl applied to the inside or outside of your windows. Use it for promotional graphics, full branding coverage, product imagery, or decorative patterns. Vivid color, sharp detail, and removable when your message changes.

Storefront · Retail · Events

Frosted & Etched Vinyl Film

Translucent vinyl that mimics the look of sandblasted or acid-etched glass at a fraction of the cost. Adds privacy while still letting light through. Ideal for conference rooms, private offices, reception areas, and bathroom windows. Can include logos, patterns, or text cut into the film.

Offices · Medical · Privacy

Perforated One-Way Film

Printed vinyl with a micro-perforated surface — opaque from outside, see-through from inside. Perfect for large storefront windows where you want maximum graphic impact without blocking the view from inside. Common in retail, restaurants, and high-traffic street-facing windows.

Storefront · Restaurants · Retail

Cut Vinyl Lettering & Logos

Single-color precision-cut vinyl text and logos applied directly to glass. The clean, professional choice for suite doors, storefront hours and contact info, office suite numbers, and any application where a simple branded element does the job without a full printed graphic.

Suite Doors · Hours · Logos

Window Film Borders & Patterns

Decorative band or stripe applications — frosted borders, geometric patterns, or logo-repeating backgrounds — that add visual interest to glass walls and partitions without full coverage. Popular in modern open offices for breaking up glass expanses while maintaining the open aesthetic.

Corporate · Open Office · Design

Safety & Manifestation Film

Solid or striped film applied at eye level to full-height glass panels to meet California building code requirements for glass manifestation. Required on floor-to-ceiling glass walls in commercial spaces — we handle specification and installation to ADA and Cal/OSHA compliance.

Safety · Code Compliance · ADA
