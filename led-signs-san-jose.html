About LED Signs

What Are LED Signs
and Why Do They Work?

LED signs cover a broad category — any sign that uses light-emitting diodes as its primary light source. In practice, that means LED channel letters (the most common illuminated storefront sign in San Jose), LED cabinet signs with backlit face panels, programmable LED message centers that display changing text and graphics, and LED open signs in windows. What they share is energy efficiency, long lifespan, and significantly better visibility than older fluorescent or neon alternatives.

LED channel letters draw roughly 50-70% less power than equivalent neon signs and last 50,000+ hours before requiring maintenance — meaning a set installed today should perform without lamp replacement for 10-15 years under normal operating conditions. The light output is consistent, color-accurate to your brand specifications, and visible in direct California sunlight, which neon struggles with.

LED message centers (programmable signs with moving text or graphics) are subject to specific City of San Jose regulations on animation speed, brightness, and content change intervals. These vary by zoning district. We handle permit submittals for LED signs through SJPermits.org, including the additional EMC-specific requirements for programmable displays.

LED Sign Options

Types of LED Signs
We Design & Install

LED Channel Letters

Individual 3D letters with LED modules inside, illuminating through a translucent acrylic face. The standard for retail and restaurant storefronts in San Jose — energy-efficient, brand-accurate color, 50,000+ hour lifespan.

LED Cabinet Signs

Aluminum cabinet with LED-backlit translucent face panel. Brighter and more energy-efficient than fluorescent cabinet signs. Common in multi-tenant retail centers and gas station price signs.

Halo-Lit LED Letters

LEDs face the wall rather than forward — the glow appears behind each letter as an architectural halo. Premium look for upscale retail, hospitality, and corporate buildings. Uses the same LED technology with a reverse-mount configuration.

LED Message Centers (EMC)

Programmable electronic message centers with full-color LED display panels. Update messaging remotely — hours, specials, events, announcements. Subject to San Jose zoning regulations on animation and brightness; we handle the EMC-specific permit process.

LED Monument Sign Faces

LED-illuminated cabinet faces for monument signs — illuminated panel inserts for multi-tenant directory monuments, entry identification signs, and property monument structures.

LED Open & Window Signs

LED open signs, window-mounted LED displays, and lightbox signs for retail windows. Bright, energy-efficient, and no permit required for most window-mounted applications.
