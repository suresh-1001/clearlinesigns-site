About Lobby Signs

What Makes a Great
Lobby Sign?

A lobby sign is often the first physical brand touchpoint a client, candidate, or visitor encounters. Before a meeting starts, before anyone speaks — your reception wall is already communicating something. Done right, it says the company is professional, established, and pays attention to detail. Done wrong, or not done at all, it's a missed opportunity every single day.

In San Jose and across Silicon Valley, we build lobby signs in a range of materials and configurations — from straightforward flat cut acrylic letters for a startup's first office, to precision-cut brushed stainless steel logo walls for corporate headquarters. The right choice depends on your brand aesthetic, wall surface, budget, and how much visual impact you want to make at the door.

As an authorized Gemini reseller, we source from the industry's most trusted dimensional sign manufacturer. Every Gemini product carries a lifetime guarantee on materials — and we stand behind the installation too.

Materials & Options

Choose Your Material —
We Handle the Rest

Every lobby sign material has a different look, feel, and price point. Here's what we offer and who each is best suited for.

Flat Cut Acrylic

Most Popular · Best Value

Precision-cut individual letters, logos, or shapes from a flat acrylic sheet. Vibrant color options, lightweight, and easy to install on almost any wall surface. A strong choice for startups, medical offices, and tech companies who want a clean, professional look without the premium price of metal.

  • Sizes from ¾" up to 95" as a single piece
  • Thicknesses: ⅛", 3/16", ¼", ⅜", ½", ¾", 1", 1½"
  • Standard, custom paint, or high-resolution print finishes
  • Colorlast UV-print option for multi-color logos
  • ADA-compliant braille available (Raised Print option)
  • Interior and exterior rated
Lifetime Guarantee — Interior & Exterior

Flat Cut Metal

Premium · High-Impact

Precision laser, router, or waterjet-cut letters and logos from real metal sheet stock. The premium option for corporate headquarters, law firms, financial institutions, and any environment where material quality sends a message on its own. Available in a full range of metals and finishes.

  • Materials: aluminum, brass, bronze, copper, stainless (304 & 316), Corten
  • Sizes from ½" up to 95" as a single piece
  • Thicknesses: ⅛", ¼", ⅜", ½", ¾", 1"
  • Brushed, polished, painted, or UV Colorlast finishes
  • Precision edges — handles the most intricate logo geometries
Lifetime Guarantee — Interior & Exterior

Lit Acrylic (Illuminated)

High Visual Impact

Precision-machined UV-stable acrylic letters with embedded energy-efficient LEDs. Available in four lighting configurations — halo lit, face lit, side lit, or combination — with a choice of metal laminate, translucent vinyl, or painted face finishes. Makes a strong statement in reception areas with lower ambient light.

  • Sizes: 8" minimum, 24" maximum per letter
  • Depth: 1½"
  • Lighting: halo lit, face lit, side lit, combo
  • LED colors: white, warm white, red, green, blue
  • Face finishes: metal laminates, translucent vinyl, painted
  • UL-certified LED lighting and power supplies
Lifetime Guarantee · 5-Year Electrical Warranty

Flat Cut PVC

Budget-Friendly · Fast Turn

Affordable, lightweight dimensional letters cut from PVC sheet stock. Inclusive pricing — paint, mounting hardware, and installation pattern are included in the base price. A smart choice for tenant suites, breakout rooms, conference rooms, and any interior space where you need professional branding without the acrylic or metal price tag.

  • Max size: 46.5" × 95"
  • Thicknesses: ¼", ⅜", ½", ¾", 1", 1½"
  • Standard, custom, and metallic painted colors — no extra cost
  • Colorlast UV-print option for multi-color designs
  • Lightweight — installs with double-face tape or stud mounting
  • Interior use only
Lifetime Guarantee — Interior Use
