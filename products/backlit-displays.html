About This Product

Graphics That Glow —
Visible in Any Lighting

Backlit displays use LED illumination behind a translucent graphic panel to produce vivid, even lighting that makes colors pop and commands attention regardless of ambient light conditions. They work in bright retail environments and dim lobbies equally well.

We supply the printed backlit film or fabric graphic and can source the LED frame hardware — from slim wall-mount lightboxes to freestanding SEG fabric frames. Custom sizes available for any installation.

  • Backlit film & SEG fabric options
  • Wall-mount & freestanding frames

Common Applications

Where Illuminated Graphics Make the Difference

Retail & Showrooms

Backlit displays are the premium product display format in retail — used by fashion brands, electronics stores, and luxury showrooms to make product visuals glow and stand apart from standard print. Consistent illumination works in bright or dim environments.

Corporate Lobbies & Reception

A backlit logo panel or branded graphic in a lobby makes an immediate impression on clients and visitors. Common in tech company headquarters, medical facilities, and professional services firms throughout Silicon Valley.

Trade Shows & Event Backdrops

SEG fabric frames with backlit graphics are increasingly common at high-end trade show booths and corporate events. The illumination makes your display visible across a crowded hall and gives it a premium finish that standard fabric backdrops can't match.

Graphic & Hardware Options

Backlit Film (Lightbox Graphic)

Translucent polyester film printed with inks optimized for backlit display. Produces vivid, saturated color when illuminated. For use in existing lightbox frames or LED panels.

Film Only

Slim LED Lightbox Frame

Ultra-thin aluminum LED frame — typically 1.5–2" deep — for wall mounting. Snap-open profile for easy graphic changes. Available in custom sizes from small accent panels to large-format walls.

Wall Mount

SEG Backlit Fabric Frame

Silicone Edge Graphic frame with dye-sublimation fabric stretched over a backlit LED frame. Fabric eliminates glare and wrinkles. Used in retail environments and premium trade show booths.

Premium

Freestanding LED Display

Double-sided freestanding backlit frame for lobby centers, showroom floors, and trade show aisle placement. Visible from both sides, available in single-panel and multi-panel configurations.
