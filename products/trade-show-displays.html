About This Product

Everything You Need for
a Professional Booth Presence

Trade show displays range from a single retractable banner to a complete branded environment with fabric backdrop, table cover, banner stands, and hanging signage. We supply individual components or full packages, depending on your budget and show requirements.

Most Silicon Valley tech companies we work with need something between the two — a backdrop, a couple of banner stands, and a table cover. That combination covers 90% of standard 10×10 booths at venues like the San Jose Convention Center or Santa Clara Convention Center.

  • Complete 10×10 packages available
  • Lightweight, portable hardware
  • Fabric and vinyl graphic options
  • Table covers in 4, 6, and 8 ft
  • Replacement graphics available
  • Bay Area delivery or ship-to-venue
