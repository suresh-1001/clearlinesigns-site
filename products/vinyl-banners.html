Common Applications

Where Vinyl Banners Deliver

Grand Openings & Retail Promotions

Draw foot traffic and announce a new location, sale, or seasonal promotion. A banner on your storefront facade or parking lot fence is still one of the highest-ROI marketing tools in local retail.

Trade Shows & Corporate Events

Step-and-repeat backdrops, booth backdrop banners, and tabletop displays all use vinyl or fabric banner material. We can supply single banners or a complete booth package for your next Bay Area event.

Outdoor Advertising & Real Estate

Fences, scaffolding, construction sites, and property listings are all common spots for large-format vinyl banners. Mesh material works best in high-wind locations; 18oz blockout works where opacity matters.

Available Options

Choose the Right Banner Material

13oz Standard Vinyl

Versatile and economical — the right choice for most indoor and short-term outdoor applications. Single-sided print with clean, vibrant output.

Most Popular

18oz Blockout Vinyl

Heavier material with a blackout layer — ideal for double-sided printing where light bleed would be an issue. Commonly used for outdoor fence banners and building wraps.

Double-Sided

Mesh Banner

Perforated vinyl that lets wind pass through — the go-to for construction fences, scaffolding, and any high-wind installation. Reduces load on attachment points.

High Wind

Fabric Banner

Soft, lightweight, and wrinkle-resistant — fabric banners produce richer color depth and a premium feel. Ideal for trade show backdrops, retail interiors, and photo opportunities.
