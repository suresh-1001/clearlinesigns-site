About This Product

The Standard for Corporate
Event Photography Backgrounds

A step-and-repeat backdrop is a large printed banner featuring a repeating logo or pattern — designed so that no matter where a photo is taken in front of it, at least one full logo is visible. It's the backdrop you see at every awards ceremony, product launch, and sponsor wall at major events.

We print on vinyl or fabric and supply adjustable telescoping stand hardware. Fabric is the better choice for events with flash photography — no glare. Vinyl works well for outdoor use and tighter budgets.

  • Standard sizes: 8×8, 8×10, 8×20
  • Fabric (no glare) or vinyl options
  • Adjustable telescoping hardware
  • Single or multi-logo layouts
  • Sponsor wall configurations
  • Carry bag included
Common Applications

Every Event Needs a Backdrop

Product Launches & Corporate Events

Every photo taken at the event includes your branding. Common at company milestones, customer summits, and executive roundtables where visibility and brand consistency matter.

Sponsor Walls & Award Ceremonies

Larger 8×20 configurations feature multiple sponsor logos in a grid layout — standard for galas, nonprofit events, and industry award ceremonies throughout the South Bay.

Trade Shows & Recruiting Booths

A step-and-repeat makes an excellent booth backdrop at trade shows and career fairs. Doubles as a photography station — attendees who take photos in front of it share your brand organically.

Sizes, Materials & Configurations

8×8 Standard

The most common size for single-company events and trade show booths. Fits a standard 10×10 booth comfortably. Vinyl or fabric.

Most Common

8×10 & 8×20 Wide

Wider formats for sponsor walls and larger events. The 8×20 is standard for galas and award ceremonies where multiple sponsors need equal logo placement.

Sponsor Walls

Fabric (No-Glare)

Dye-sublimation printed fabric eliminates the reflective glare you get with vinyl under flash photography. The right choice for any event where photos will be taken.

Best for Photos

Vinyl (Budget Option)

13oz or 18oz vinyl is a more economical alternative when glare isn't a concern — good for outdoor events and booths where photography isn't the primary use.
