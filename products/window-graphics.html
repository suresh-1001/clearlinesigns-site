Common Applications

Every Business Has Windows Worth Using

Storefronts & Retail

Business name, logo, hours, phone number, and promotional messaging. Cut vinyl is the cleanest option for lettering; full-color printed film is the go-to for bold graphics and photography.

Office Privacy & Branding

Frosted vinyl film adds privacy to glass conference rooms and offices without blocking natural light. It also takes printed logos and patterns — a popular choice for tech companies that want branded interiors without permanent modifications.

Large-Format & Perforated Film

Perforated window film (one-way vision) covers large glass surfaces with full-color graphics while maintaining visibility from inside. Common on commercial buildings, vehicles, and high-visibility retail windows.

Available Options

Choose the Right Window Film

Cut Vinyl Lettering

Single-color vinyl cut to shape — business name, logo, phone, hours. Clean, durable, and the most economical window graphic option. Easy to remove and update.

Most Economical

Full-Color Printed Film

High-resolution printed vinyl for logos, photography, and complex graphics. Applied to interior or exterior glass surfaces. Available in gloss, matte, and opaque finishes.

Full Color

Frosted & Etched Film

Translucent vinyl that mimics sandblasted or etched glass. Great for conference rooms, shower doors, and interior partitions. Can be printed with logos and patterns.

Privacy

Perforated One-Way Vision

Micro-perforated film that shows graphics from outside while allowing visibility from inside. Ideal for large storefront windows and vehicle glass where you don't want to block the view.
