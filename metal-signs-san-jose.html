About Metal Signs

Custom Metal Signs in San Jose —
What They Are & When to Use Them

Metal signs are the permanent end of the sign spectrum. Where vinyl and acrylic are the right call for most interior branding, metal is what you reach for when the sign needs to convey institutional permanence — a building entrance, a donor wall, a corporate lobby, an outdoor monument. Cast aluminum, brushed stainless steel, and brass each carry a visual weight that no other material matches.

In San Jose's commercial market, metal signs appear most often as dimensional letters on a building or lobby wall, cast aluminum plaques at building entrances or in memorial applications, stainless steel suite and room identification signs, and custom metal fabrications for corporate identity programs. Most exterior metal signs require a city permit — we handle the full submittal process.

Material choice matters. Cast aluminum is the workhorse — foundry-poured, corrosion-resistant, available in a wide range of finishes, and backed by a lifetime warranty through Gemini Sign Products. Stainless steel reads as contemporary and architectural. Brass carries a classic, formal weight preferred in law firms, medical offices, and institutional settings. We'll recommend the right metal for your application, budget, and environment.

How It Works

From Material Selection
to Final Installation

Metal sign projects follow a predictable sequence. Here's what to expect.

1
Step One

Consultation & Material Selection

We discuss your application, environment, and budget. We'll recommend the right metal — cast aluminum, stainless, brass — and show you finish samples. If it's an exterior sign, we pull the applicable zoning requirements for your San Jose address at this stage.

2
Step Two

Design & Proof Approval

We produce a scaled rendering with exact dimensions, letter heights, finish callouts, and mounting detail. For cast aluminum plaques, we show the layout before the mold is set. You approve the design before anything goes into production.

3
Step Three

Fabrication & Permits

Production runs 3–6 weeks depending on material and complexity. Cast aluminum plaques are 3–4 weeks standard. Custom fabrications run longer. If a permit is required, we submit concurrent with production to minimize overall timeline.

4
Step Four

Professional Installation

Metal signs require precise mounting — correct anchor points for the substrate, proper standoff spacing, level alignment. Our installers handle the full job, including any wall patching or touch-up required after mounting hardware is set.
