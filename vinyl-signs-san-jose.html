About Vinyl Signs

What Are Vinyl Signs
and What Can They Do?

Vinyl signs cover a wide range of commercial signage — anywhere adhesive-backed or static-cling film is applied to a surface. That includes cut vinyl lettering on storefront windows and doors, full-color printed graphics on walls and floors, window films for privacy or branding, vinyl banners for events and promotions, and large-format graphics applied to building exteriors or vehicles. It's one of the most cost-effective sign categories in commercial signage, and for most applications, no permit is required.

Not all vinyl is the same. Outdoor applications — storefront windows facing south or west, building-mounted graphics, banner hardware installations — need cast vinyl rated for 5–7 years of UV exposure, not the economy calendered material you'll find at a print shop. We use commercial-grade 3M and Avery Dennison films with appropriate overlaminates for outdoor applications, and pressure-sensitive films from the same suppliers for interior work. The material spec matters as much as the print quality.

Installation is where vinyl signs succeed or fail. Air pockets, misaligned graphics, and peeling edges are almost always an installation problem, not a material problem. Our installers work with wet and dry application methods depending on surface type and graphic size, and we use professional-grade squeegees and heat guns to get a clean, lasting bond on glass, painted walls, aluminum, and most other commercial surfaces.

Cut Vinyl Lettering

Precision-cut single-color vinyl letters and logos applied directly to windows, walls, doors, and vehicles. No background — just the lettering itself, clean and professional. The go-to for storefront window hours, business names on glass, and vehicle lettering.

Storefront · Vehicles · Windows

Printed Vinyl Graphics

Full-color printed graphics on adhesive vinyl — for window displays, wall graphics, floor decals, and promotional signage. Produced on wide-format inkjet printers with UV-stable inks and finished with a protective overlam for durability.

Full Color · Indoor & Outdoor

Window Graphics & Film

Perforated window film for one-way visibility, frosted vinyl for privacy, full-color window wraps, and cut vinyl hours and contact information. Applied to storefront glass, office partitions, and entry doors. No permit required in most cases.

Storefront · Privacy · Branding

Wall Graphics & Murals

Large-format printed vinyl applied to office and retail walls — brand murals, values statements, environmental graphics, and wayfinding. Installed on painted drywall, brick, and most commercial wall surfaces. Removable without damage.

Office · Retail · Lobby

Vinyl Banners

Hemmed and grommeted 13oz or 18oz vinyl banners for outdoor events, grand openings, construction fencing, and promotions. Single or double-sided. Standard and custom sizes with same-week turnaround available for most orders.

Events · Outdoor · Fast Turn

Floor Graphics & Decals

Anti-slip laminated vinyl graphics for retail floors, trade show booths, lobbies, and wayfinding applications. Rated for foot traffic with a textured overlam that meets commercial slip-resistance requirements. Removable and replaceable.

Retail · Trade Show · Wayfinding

Right Material for the Application

Economy vinyl fails outdoors — it shrinks, fades, and peels within a year on a south-facing window. We spec the right film for every job: cast vinyl for outdoor and vehicle applications, calendered film for short-term interior work, floor-rated overlam for foot-traffic areas. We'll tell you what the job actually needs, not just what prints fastest.
