About Custom Decals

Custom Vinyl Decals —
Small Format, Wide Application

Decals and vinyl stickers serve a different purpose than large-format signage — they go where a sign can't. On product packaging, laptop lids, hard hats, vehicle bumpers, equipment cases, water bottles, and storefront glass. For San Jose businesses, custom decals are a cost-effective way to extend brand visibility into the hands and spaces of your customers without a large investment.

The distinction between a decal and a sticker is mostly format. Die-cut decals are trimmed to the exact outline of your artwork — no rectangular background, just your logo or shape. Standard stickers keep a defined edge (rectangle, circle, square). Clear vinyl stickers appear to float on the surface they're applied to. All are printed with UV-resistant inks and laminated for durability, whether they're going on a car bumper, a product jar, or a window that gets direct California sun.

We produce decals for individual businesses and small runs without requiring bulk minimums. If you need a handful of logo decals for a trade show or a few hundred product labels for a product launch, we handle both. Turnaround on most decal orders is 3–5 business days, with rush options available.

Decal Options

Types of Custom Decals
We Print & Cut

Die-Cut Vinyl Decals

Cut to the exact contour of your logo or artwork. No background — just your shape. Available on white, clear, or metallic vinyl in any size from 1 inch to several feet.

Clear Vinyl Stickers

Printed on transparent vinyl so the surface shows through the unprinted areas. Creates a "printed directly on the surface" look — popular for window applications and product packaging.

Logo & Brand Decals

Your logo reproduced accurately in full color on durable vinyl — for equipment, helmets, vehicles, trade show giveaways, and any surface you want to brand.

Product Labels & Stickers

Custom labels for jars, bottles, boxes, and packaging. Waterproof vinyl options for food, beverage, and cosmetic products that need to survive refrigeration or handling.

Window Decals

Static-cling or adhesive-backed decals for storefront glass — hours, logos, certifications, social handles, and promotional messaging that applies and removes without residue.

Bumper Stickers & Vehicle Decals

UV-resistant outdoor vinyl rated for years of vehicle and outdoor use. Printed with fade-resistant inks and laminated for protection against California sun and car washes.
