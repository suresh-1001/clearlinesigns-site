About Window Decals

What Are Window Decals
and How Are They Different?

Window decals are individual cut or printed vinyl graphics applied directly to glass — logos, business names, hours of operation, phone numbers, regulatory notices, and promotional messaging. They're different from full window wraps or frosted privacy film (which cover the entire pane) because decals are targeted: one graphic, one message, applied precisely where you want it.

For San Jose businesses, window decals are one of the fastest, most affordable ways to brand a storefront or office. They don't require a sign permit in most cases, they go up in minutes, and they last 5–7 years outdoors with UV-resistant vinyl. When your hours change, your branding updates, or a promotion ends, decals peel off clean without damaging the glass.

We produce window decals in cut vinyl (solid colors, clean edges, no background), full-color printed vinyl (photos and gradients), and frosted/etched vinyl (privacy effect with a branded logo or pattern). Interior or exterior application depending on your glass type and exposure.

Decal Options

Types of Window Decals
We Design & Install

Cut Vinyl Decals

Computer-cut from solid-color vinyl — clean edges, no background. The standard for logos, business names, hours, and phone numbers on storefront glass. Available in dozens of colors and metallic finishes.

Printed Vinyl Decals

Full-color digital print on adhesive vinyl. Ideal for photo-quality graphics, product images, gradients, and multi-color logos that can't be produced with cut vinyl alone.

Frosted / Etched Decals

Translucent vinyl that mimics sandblasted or acid-etched glass. Adds privacy while letting light through. Used for conference rooms, medical offices, and storefronts that want an upscale frosted look with a branded logo cutout.

Perforated Window Decals

One-way vision vinyl with micro-perforations — full-color graphic on the outside, see-through from the inside. Common for restaurant windows, retail promotions, and vehicle rear windows.

Static Cling Decals

Non-adhesive vinyl that clings to glass without glue. Easy to reposition and remove — good for seasonal promotions, temporary notices, and retail windows that change messaging frequently.

Die-Cut Stickers & Labels

Custom-shaped stickers for product packaging, equipment labeling, giveaways, and brand swag. Cut to any contour with laminated UV protection for indoor or outdoor use.
