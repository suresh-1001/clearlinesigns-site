About Pop-Up Displays

What Are Pop-Up Displays
and Why Do Exhibitors Use Them?

A pop-up display is a portable, self-contained trade show backdrop that expands from a compact carry bag into a full-width exhibit wall in minutes — no tools, no hardware, no installation crew. The aluminum or fiberglass frame unfolds and locks into shape, then the fabric graphic or panel system attaches to create a seamless, professional-looking display that fills your 8, 10, or 20-foot booth space.

Pop-up displays are the workhorse of Silicon Valley's conference and trade show circuit. They're used by tech companies at trade shows like RSA, VMworld, and Cisco Live, by healthcare companies at medical conferences, and by any business that needs a polished exhibit presence without the weight and cost of a custom booth build. The same display ships to multiple shows, stores in a closet between events, and can be re-skinned with new graphics when the campaign changes.

Modern pop-up displays use dye-sublimation fabric printing — the same process used for SEG backlit displays — which produces vivid, seamless graphics without panel joints breaking up the image. Backlit versions add an LED lighting system inside the frame that illuminates the fabric from behind for a glowing, high-impact look on the show floor.

Display Options

Types of Pop-Up Displays
We Design & Print

Fabric SEG Pop-Up Display

Aluminum expanding frame with silicone edge graphic (SEG) fabric that tucks into channels on the frame for a taut, seamless look. Available in 8ft, 10ft, and 20ft widths — curved or straight. The current standard for professional trade show exhibits.

Backlit Pop-Up Display

SEG fabric pop-up with integrated LED lighting behind the graphic panel. The fabric diffuses the light evenly across the entire display for a glowing effect that stands out on any show floor. Available in 8ft and 10ft widths — requires standard 120V outlet access.

Velcro Panel Pop-Up Display

Traditional pop-up frame with velcro-receptive fabric surface panels. Graphics print on rigid substrate tiles that attach directly to the frame — easy to swap individual panels for different shows or updated messaging without replacing the entire graphic.

Curved Pop-Up Display

Convex curved frame design that adds depth and dimension to your booth space — the curved shape draws the eye and differentiates your display from flat backwall competitors. Available in fabric SEG and velcro panel configurations.

Tabletop Pop-Up Display

Compact 4ft or 6ft pop-up sized for tabletop use — conference registration tables, product demo stations, and small event booths where a full-height backwall isn't needed. Sets up in under two minutes and fits in a carry bag.

Pop-Up Display with Counter

Full-height pop-up backwall bundled with a matching branded counter — folding or wheeled — for a complete, coordinated booth setup. Counter provides space for literature, product demos, and customer interaction in front of the display.
