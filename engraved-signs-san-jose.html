About Engraved Signs

Engraved Signs — When Permanence
Is Part of the Message

Engraved signs communicate something that printed vinyl and backlit displays can't — permanence. A cast aluminum plaque on a building entrance, an engraved acrylic name plate on a reception desk, or a sandblasted stone-look sign at a corporate headquarters tells visitors this organization is established and serious. For San Jose and Santa Clara businesses competing in a market full of tech startups and fast-moving tenants, that signal matters.

Engraved signage covers a broad range of products and materials. Cast aluminum plaques are the traditional standard for building identification, commemorative signs, and official-looking exterior displays — the kind of sign that looks like it's been there for decades because it has been, or because it's built to be. Engraved acrylic signs offer a modern alternative: sharp, clean, and suitable for interior office environments where a metal plaque would feel too heavy. Sandblasted HDU (High-Density Urethane) gives the look of carved wood or stone without the weight or maintenance.

We source cast aluminum plaques through Gemini Sign Products, one of the most respected manufacturers in the industry, offering a lifetime warranty on their cast products. For ADA-compliant engraved room signs — tactile text, Grade 2 Braille, and matte finishes — we produce to California Title 24 specifications and can handle full building programs with consistent finishes across every room.

Sign Options

Types of Engraved Signs
We Design & Fabricate

Cast Aluminum Plaques

The gold standard for building identification, commemorative plaques, and official exterior signage. Available in natural aluminum, bronze, or painted finishes with raised or recessed lettering and artwork. Lifetime warranty through Gemini Sign Products.

Engraved Acrylic Signs

Precision-routed acrylic panels with filled lettering in any color — a clean, modern alternative to metal for interior office name plates, door signs, and directional panels. Durable, lightweight, and professional.

ADA Engraved Room Signs

Tactile text, Grade 2 Braille, and pictogram panels for room identification in compliance with California Title 24 and ADA Title III. Available in a full range of substrate colors and finishes.

Metal Name Plates & Desk Signs

Engraved brass, aluminum, or stainless steel name plates for office doors, cubicle panels, and reception desks. Individual pieces or full office programs — consistent finish across every location.

Sandblasted & Carved Signs

Sandblasted HDU panels that replicate the look of hand-carved wood or stone — deep-relief lettering and artwork with a dimensional quality that flat signs can't match. Suitable for exterior monument bases and building entries.

Commemorative & Recognition Plaques

Donor walls, award plaques, dedication signs, and recognition panels for corporations, nonprofits, schools, and government facilities. Cast aluminum or engraved metal with custom artwork and mounting hardware.
