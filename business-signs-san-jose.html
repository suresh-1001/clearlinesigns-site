About Business Signs

Custom Business Signs
Built for San Jose

Business signs do more than display your name — they set first impressions, guide customers through your space, and reinforce your brand at every touchpoint. In a dense commercial market like San Jose, the right signage is the difference between getting noticed and getting passed by.

The term "business signs" covers a wide range: exterior identification like channel letters and monument signs, interior lobby signs and dimensional lettering, wayfinding and ADA-compliant room signs, window graphics, and everything in between. Most businesses need a combination of sign types rather than a single solution. We assess your location, your lease restrictions, and the City of San Jose's sign code to recommend what will work — and what will get approved.

We've completed 500+ sign projects for businesses across Silicon Valley — from tech companies in North San Jose to retail shops along The Alameda and restaurant corridors in Santana Row and Willow Glen. Every project is managed by our local team from first proof to final installation.
