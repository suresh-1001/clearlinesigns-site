About Acrylic Signs

What Makes Acrylic Signs
the Interior Standard

Acrylic shows up everywhere in commercial signage because it's genuinely versatile — not just as a default. It's available in cast or extruded form, in thicknesses from ⅛" to 1½", in dozens of standard colors, and in clear, frosted, mirrored, or matte finishes. Unlike metal, it can be backlit. Unlike PVC, it holds a polished edge. Unlike vinyl, it has physical depth. That combination is why it's the most specified interior sign material in Silicon Valley offices.

Cast acrylic — the commercial standard — is optically clearer, more dimensionally stable, and significantly more durable than extruded acrylic from hardware stores. On a backlit panel, the difference between cast and extruded is visible: cast distributes light more evenly with fewer hot spots. We use commercial cast acrylic exclusively, fabricated in-house with routed edges and precision drilling for standoff hardware.

Acrylic signs are used across a wider range of applications than most clients expect: reception walls, wayfinding panels, room ID plaques (ADA-compliant when combined with Braille and tactile text), donor recognition walls, display cases, conference room signs, and exterior plaques on low-traffic surfaces. Sign Options

Acrylic Sign Types
We Design & Install

Lobby & Reception Signs

Logo and company name precision-cut from thick-cast acrylic, mounted to a painted backer panel or directly to the wall with standoffs. The most requested interior sign type for San Jose offices and tech companies.

Backlit Acrylic Panels

LED-illuminated acrylic panels for logos, reception walls, and interior feature displays. Light distributes evenly behind the face panel for a premium glowing effect without channel letter complexity.

Frosted & Etched Acrylic

Frosted acrylic panels for privacy screens, conference room walls, and decorative interior applications. Etched or digitally printed frosting for logos and patterns on glass-adjacent surfaces.

Dimensional Acrylic Letters

Individual letters routed from thick acrylic and mounted with standoffs for a floating 3D effect. Available in any color, gloss or matte finish. Common for lobby feature walls and building directories.

ADA-Compliant Room Signs

Acrylic room identification signs with tactile text, Grade 2 Braille, and pictograms per California Title 24 and ADA standards. Required for most commercial office build-outs in San Jose.

Clear & Colored Acrylic Displays

Custom display panels, product holders, and interior graphic frames in clear, colored, or mirrored acrylic. Common for retail environments, trade show displays, and corporate showrooms.
