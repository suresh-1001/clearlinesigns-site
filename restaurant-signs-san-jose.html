About Restaurant Signs

What Restaurant Signs
Actually Need to Do

A restaurant sign has one job before anything else: stop foot traffic. Whether you're on a busy San Jose street, inside Santana Row, or anchoring a strip mall on Stevens Creek, your exterior sign is the first conversion point — the moment a potential customer decides to walk in or keep walking.

Restaurant signage in San Jose spans a wide range of formats. Channel letter sets are the most common exterior choice for sit-down restaurants and cafes — illuminated, brand-colored, high-visibility day and night. Cabinet signs work well in shopping centers where landlord sign criteria require uniform sizing. Blade signs projecting perpendicular from the building face are effective on pedestrian corridors where foot traffic walks parallel to the storefronts.

Inside, restaurants need menu boards, wayfinding to restrooms and exits, window graphics for promotions, and often vinyl banners for seasonal offers or event nights. We handle all of it — and we know San Jose's sign permit process for restaurant tenants, including the landlord approval packages that most commercial leases require before fabrication begins. Permit submittals go through SJPermits.org.

Sign Options

Restaurant Sign Types
We Design & Install

Channel Letter Signs

Individual illuminated 3D letters mounted to the building fascia or raceway. The most common exterior sign for restaurants in San Jose — high visibility, full brand color control, available front-lit, halo-lit, or combination.

Cabinet Signs (Box Signs)

Aluminum cabinet with illuminated translucent face panel. Common in strip malls and shopping centers where landlord criteria require consistent sign sizes across all tenant bays.

Blade & Projecting Signs

Signs that extend perpendicular from the building — visible to foot traffic walking along the sidewalk rather than approaching head-on. Effective in San Jose's downtown and Santana Row corridors.

Window Graphics & Decals

Full-color window graphics, frosted vinyl, and promotional decals for specials, hours, and seasonal offers. Fast to produce and change — ideal for rotating promotions without requiring a new permit.

Menu Boards & Interior Signs

Printed or dimensional menu boards, directional signs to restrooms, and branded wall graphics that carry your identity from the sidewalk all the way to the table.

Vinyl Banners & A-Frames

High-quality vinyl banners for grand openings, events, and seasonal promotions. A-frame sidewalk signs for daily specials and foot traffic capture — no permit required for most placements.
