About Wall Graphics

What Wall Graphics Can Do
for Your Space

Wall graphics transform blank interior surfaces into branded, functional, or inspiring environments. For a Silicon Valley tech company, that might mean a floor-to-ceiling company timeline mural in the main corridor, frosted vinyl values statements on conference room glass, or a large-format logo wall in the all-hands space. For a medical office, it might be calming nature imagery in the waiting area or clear wayfinding graphics down hallways.

The technology behind wall graphics has improved dramatically. Modern printable wall vinyl adheres cleanly to painted drywall, concrete, brick, wood paneling, and even textured surfaces — and removable-adhesive options mean you can update graphics when your brand evolves or your lease ends without damaging the underlying wall.

Unlike building signs, interior wall graphics don't require a City of San Jose sign permit for most applications. That means faster execution, lower total cost, and the ability to refresh your space without a months-long approval process.

One of the Highest-Impact, Lowest-Friction Sign Types Available

No structural work, no permit, no landlord sign criteria review. A full wall mural can be designed, printed, and installed in under two weeks. It's one of the fastest ways to make a dramatic visual change to a commercial space — and one of the most cost-effective per square foot of visual impact.

We Design for the Wall, Not a Screen

A graphic that looks good at 1080px looks very different stretched across a 12-foot wall. We design wall graphics at true print scale — accounting for viewing distance, lighting conditions, wall texture, and how the graphic interacts with the surrounding space. No pixelation, no color surprises, no reprints.

Commercial-Grade Materials

We use cast and calendered vinyl rated for interior wall applications — not the peel-and-stick rolls from a craft store. Proper wall vinyl adheres cleanly, resists fading under office lighting, and removes cleanly at end of lease without pulling paint. The difference between a three-year installation and one that's bubbling in six months.

Surface Assessment Before Every Job

Not all walls take vinyl the same way. Freshly painted walls need cure time. Textured surfaces need specific adhesive profiles. Concrete and brick require a different application approach than smooth drywall. We assess before we quote — no mid-job surprises about surface compatibility.

Fast Turnaround — Typically Under Two Weeks

From approved design to installed graphics, most wall graphic projects complete in 7–12 business days. No permit process, no structural work, no landlord approval package required for interior wall applications. One of the fastest-moving sign types we install.
