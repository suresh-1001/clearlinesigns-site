About Trade Show Displays

Trade Show Displays Built
for Bay Area Companies

San Jose sits at the center of one of the most event-dense regions in the country. Between the San Jose Convention Center, SAP Center, and venues throughout Silicon Valley, Bay Area companies exhibit at dozens of trade shows, expos, and corporate events every year. Your booth display is often the first physical impression a prospect has of your brand — it needs to hold up to that.

Trade show displays range from a single retractable banner stand at a tabletop exhibit to a full 10x10 or 10x20 booth with a fabric backdrop, counter, and printed table cover. Most display hardware is designed to pack into a carry bag or shipping case and set up without tools in under 10 minutes. The graphics — fabric or vinyl — are what carry your brand, and they need to be printed sharp, color-accurate, and sized precisely to the hardware they're going on.

We handle design, print, and hardware for the full range of trade show display products. If you already have hardware, we can produce replacement graphics to fit. If you're starting from scratch, we'll spec a setup that fits your booth size, budget, and event schedule.

Display Options

Types of Trade Show Displays
We Design & Produce

Retractable Banner Stands

The most portable display option — banner retracts into the base for easy transport. Available in standard (33"), wide (47"), and extra-wide formats. Fast to set up, easy to store, and graphic-swappable.

Fabric Pop-Up Displays

Lightweight aluminum frame with a tension fabric graphic that zips on or wraps around. Clean, seamless look with no visible hardware. Folds into a carrying bag and sets up in minutes.

Step & Repeat Backdrops

Branded event backdrops for press appearances, product launches, award shows, and corporate events. Available with adjustable stand hardware and carry cases. Fabric or vinyl print options.

Table Covers & Throws

Fitted or draped fabric table covers with full-color dye-sublimation printing. Fitted covers look sharp and stay put during busy show hours. Machine washable and reusable.

Backlit Displays

LED-illuminated fabric displays for maximum visibility on a crowded show floor. Backlit graphics draw attention in any lighting environment and photograph well for social media coverage.

Tabletop Displays

Compact displays for smaller booth footprints — folding tabletop pop-ups, A-frame displays, and rigid panel displays that sit on a table and still project a professional brand presence.
